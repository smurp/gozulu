We are looking down at the top of the Earth, with Greenwich England always at the top. Greenwich Mean Time (GMT aka ZULU) is displayed above the globe. The little yellow Sun moves around the Earth in real time. The position of the Sun shows where Noon is. When the Sun is at the top, that means it is Noon in Greenwich. The daylight and night sides of the Earth are shown. Your own local time appears appears in green but you can drag it to a different timezone.
